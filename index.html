About

CEO of Switchboard Live (founded 2016) — a SaaS platform I've scaled over the past decade. Along the way, I've learned what actually works in startup operations, product development, go-to-market strategy, and the hard lessons that separate ideas from sustainable businesses.

My mission is to leverage AI and agentic tools to compress the idea-to-execution cycle—turning 18-month problems into 18-day experiments. I'm documenting and sharing the tactical frameworks I've built, helping other founders skip the expensive mistakes I've already made.

I'm currently exploring the intersection of AI agents, automation, and product development through hands-on side projects. Each experiment teaches me what works, what breaks, and how to effectively orchestrate tools to build better tools.
